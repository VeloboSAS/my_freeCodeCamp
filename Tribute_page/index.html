Alexander Bolshunov

One of the Greatest Skiers of our time.

Here's a time line of A.Bolshunov's life:

  • 1996 - Born in Bryansk region, Russia
  • 2017 - Study at the Institute of Physical Culture and Sports of Penza State University
  • 2017/2018 - Two-time world youth champion
  • 2018/2019 - First wins and Olympic record
  • 2019/2020 - Russia's first ever World Cup winner, winner of the Tour de Ski
  • 2020/2021 - World Cup winner, winner of the Tour de Ski, World champion
